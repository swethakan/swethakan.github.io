Newsgames aren't easy but when they strike, they strike true. This game was highly successful and detailed the process of battling a dishonest cop in California court. The state keeps tight security over officer personal records makign it difficult for defendants to use this information in court. With five other reporters, I introduced a new way to tell stories to the newsroom and orchestrated a massive effort to get this unconventional 'story' published. It helped very much that Len de Groot, my supervisor at the time, was supportive.
