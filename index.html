Can you win in this system?

This newsgame sucessfully detailed the process of battling a dishonest cop in California court. The state kept tight security over officer personal records making it difficult for defendants to use this information in court. With five other reporters, I introduced a new way to tell stories to the newsroom and orchestrated a massive effort to get this unconventional 'story' published. Len de Groot, my supervisor at the time, was supportive of the initiative. Read the story.
